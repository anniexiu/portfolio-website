My Story

Hi, I’m Annie. I’m a UX designer with a background in mental health and research. My background in psychology helps me understand human behavior and cognitive processes and helps me become more empathetic when I communicate and listen to my clients. I offer my experiences in conducting research studies to provide a better understanding of our users and to create useful and usable designs. Moving forward, I want to merge my background in psychology and UX design to help integrate digital products into people’s lives that are both positive and intuitive to use.
